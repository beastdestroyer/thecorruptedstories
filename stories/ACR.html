Normal lives turn grim, as the results of an unexpected event trash the world in ways deemed unimaginable. Itsuzaki Yoshino, your average high-schooler; your average go-to perv Fugayaka Tomoya; the expressive but mature Nimiro Tatsumi, and the silent but nerdy Syenshi Kotomi are all trapped amidst the chaos. As the world around them crumbles, who will rise up and destroy this Corrupted Reality? And who will fall in doing so?
